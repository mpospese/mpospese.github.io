Muottas Muragl

3 Aug 2024

This weekend we are visiting Sils Maria in the Engadin region of eastern Switzerland. Today I hiked from the funicular station of Muottas Muragl to Lej (Lake) Muragl: 7.76km, 397m ascent, 2h45m, 15°C ⛅️. It was a lovely hike that reached a maximum altitude of 2,750m. I saw cows, horses, and a marmot (and many fellow hikers).
