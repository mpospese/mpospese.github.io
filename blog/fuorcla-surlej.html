Fuorcla Surlej

4 Aug 2024

We've been here before, but the Fuorcla Surlej is always worth another visit if you're in the area. It's a short out-and-back hike from the Corvatsch cable car's Murtèl middle station, but the relatively high altitude (2,755m) always keeps the walk interesting for me: 4.59 km, 143m ascent, 1h8m, 11°C ⛅️.
