It's Grittibänz Season Already

14 November 2024

I was feeling a bit down this afternoon, so I decided to walk out to a local cafe for a cup of coffee and maybe a sweet treat. To my surprise it's already Grittibänz season. Grittibänz are bread men made from sweet dough. They are traditional in December around Saint Nicholas Day (Recipe).

It seems like the internet (and perhaps real life) is full of bad stuff lately. Be kind to yourself and don't forget to give yourself a little treat every now and then.
