Just a Little Bit of Mist

28 Aug 2024

... over Lake Ägeri this morning. The mist forms occasionally when the water is warmer than the air (a necessary but not sufficient condition). Today was my third day in a row of waking up early and getting a quick 30-minute paddle in before work, but it was the first day with some evaporation fog despite the other two days being colder.
