Morning Walk with Chains

25 November 2024

Returned to Raten today for a shorter walk just wearing chains instead of snowshoes: 3.15km, 96m ascent, 0h:47m, 8°C ☀️. Bus service comes every 30 minutes, so I wanted to keep the walk to under an hour. The return bus conveniently drops me off in front of a coffeeshop and the grocery store, so I was able to fuel up with coffee and do some pre-Thanksgiving grocery shopping.
