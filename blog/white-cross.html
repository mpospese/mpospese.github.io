White Cross

14 May 2024

Hiked up to the White Cross (Weisses Kreuz) this morning for the first time in four and a half months. Fantastic views as always, especially for 40 minutes from my front door. I should do this hike more often (it's harder than my typical farm field walks and takes a bit more time).
