Weeds

2 May 2024

The apartment we rent has a terrace. The part of the terrace that isn't wood decking or planters is covered in gravel. Despite a lack of soil, we're continually battling weeds that sprout up from the gravel. Fortunately they're easy to pull because there's not much for the roots to cling to.

This Spring though we were happy to discover that many of the "weeds" are actually arugula! We grew arugula in our raised garden beds last year and let it go to seed, and this is the result. Last year we found oregano growing wild amongst the hedges in the planters.
