First Snowshoe Hike of the Season

24 November 2024

The snowfall was two days ago and it was quite warm today, but the snow was still in decent condition and the terrain pretty.

Raten to Bellevue (and back): 4.97 km, 144 m ascent, 1h:22m, 7°C ⛅️

... and there was actually a view from Bellevue today (towards Lake Zürich).
