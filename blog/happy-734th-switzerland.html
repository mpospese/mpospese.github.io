Happy Swiss National Day!

I hiked up to the White Cross above town and then upon my return picked up coffee and the traditional 1.-August Weggen bread rolls.

Tonight we'll enjoy a cheese fondue for dinner. (Despite it being summer, it's actually been fondue weather recently.)

Same as last year, I'm grateful that I'm on a local contract and get local Swiss holidays off.
